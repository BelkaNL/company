Terahertz Testing Facility for ESA JUICE Mission

The Low Temperature Near Field THz Chamber (LORENTZ), is the newest antenna test facility developed for ESA. It is unique in that it can test antennas in the frequency range of 50-1500GHz in a realistic space environment. The main vacuum anechoic chamber can accommodate instruments with a volume of 1900mm x 1900mm with a mass up to 500kg. The antenna under test can be temperature controlled from 80-390 K (-190c â€“ 117 c) with future plans to go down to 4k (-270 c). Then through a novel design, the antenna pattern can be measured using a 1x1m planner scanner which remains at room temperature. This setup allows for the full end to end measurement of instruments in a truly representative environment.
